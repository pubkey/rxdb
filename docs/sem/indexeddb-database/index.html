Online is Optional

RxDB adopts an offline-first approach, ensuring your app remains fully functional by storing data locally on the Browser client and seamlessly syncing in the background. You can even run your app without a backend at all.

  • Keep your Browser app running offline
  • Run local queries with zero latency
  • Simplify and speed up development
  • Reduces backend load and scales better
