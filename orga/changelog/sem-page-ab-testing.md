Add per-page A/B testing for the search-engine-marketing landing pages in `docs-src/src/pages/sem/*`. Each SEM page can now A/B-test its hero title, hero paragraph, meta title, meta description and hero bullets by wrapping a field value in the new `ab(...)` helper. The assigned variant is persisted per page-id in localStorage and tagged onto the tracking events so variants can be compared in Google Analytics. This also revives the previously unused `SemPage.text` field (the hero paragraph was hardcoded) and adds configurable `metaDescription` and `bullets` fields. Related to the SEM landing page optimization work.
